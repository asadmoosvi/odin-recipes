Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is made by layering cooked lasagna noodles with alternating layers of a rich meat or vegetable sauce, cheese (typically ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan), and a creamy béchamel or tomato-based sauce. The meat sauce often consists of ground beef or Italian sausage cooked with onions, garlic, and tomatoes, while the cheese mixture is sometimes mixed with eggs and herbs like parsley. Once the layers are built, the lasagna is baked in the oven until bubbly and golden on top, allowing the flavors to meld and the cheese to melt perfectly. It is then rested briefly before serving.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles (12-16 pieces, cooked)
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage (1-1.5 lbs)
  • Onion (1, diced)
  • Garlic (2-3 cloves, minced)
  • Crushed tomatoes (28 oz can)
  • Tomato paste (6 oz can)
  • Tomato sauce (15 oz can)
  • Water (1/2 cup)
  • Ricotta cheese (15 oz)
  • Mozzarella cheese (4 cups, shredded)
  • Parmesan cheese (1/2 cup, grated)
  • Eggs (2, beaten)
  • Fresh parsley (2 tablespoons, chopped)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Pepper (to taste)
  • Dried basil (1 teaspoon)
  • Dried oregano (1 teaspoon)
  • Olive oil (for sautéing)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles in salted water according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. Prepare the meat sauce:
    • In a large pan, heat olive oil and sauté the diced onion until softened.
    • Add minced garlic and cook for about 1 minute.
    • Add the ground beef or Italian sausage, cook until browned, breaking it apart with a spoon. Drain excess fat.
    • Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, water, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer the sauce for 20-30 minutes to develop flavor.
  4. Mix the cheese filling:
    • In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, 2 beaten eggs, chopped parsley, and 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan. Season with a pinch of salt and pepper.
  5. Assemble the lasagna:
    • Spread a thin layer of meat sauce at the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
    • Lay 3-4 cooked lasagna noodles on top.
    • Spread 1/3 of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles.
    • Sprinkle a layer of shredded mozzarella cheese.
    • Top with 1/3 of the meat sauce.
    • Repeat these layers (noodles, ricotta, mozzarella, sauce) two more times.
    • For the final layer, top with noodles, remaining sauce, and sprinkle with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes, until the lasagna is bubbly and the cheese is melted and golden.
  7. Let it rest for 10-15 minutes before serving to allow the layers to set.
